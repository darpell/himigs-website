About

Here’s our little story.

  • March 2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Himig Singers, formerly called Himig Andreano, consists mostly of alumni of St. Andrew’s School in Paranaque City. The group started as guest singers at a choral conducting recital held in St. Scholastica’s College last March 2011. They performed together with Coro Andreano, the official high school choir of St. Andrew’s School. The group is open for all male singers who have the same passion for choral music and lasting commitment to serve.

  • May 2011

    The Choir is Formed

    Due to consistent and constant member participation during the summer of 2011, the group decided to formalize on May 2011 and started to serve at the Cathedral Parish of St. Andrew Para√±aque.

  • December 2012

    First Wedding Performance

    HimigS first wedding performance which was held at St. James the Great Parish Church in Ayala, Alabang.

  • October 2014

    Himigs performs during the Living Rosary

    The group performed “Oyayi”, lyrics by Chito Beltran, music by Steven Zabala and Francisco Garcia, and a world premiere arrangement by Chris Balintona, the group’s resident arranger and choirmaster.

  • January 2015

    Another Wedding Performance

    HimigS was blessed with another wedding engagement held at the Presentation of the Child Jesus Parish in BF Homes Paranaque City.

  • Present

    Group Continues to sing

    At present, HimigS continues to serve at Masses as a church-based, all-male ensemble. They are also currently under the umbrella of Cathedral Parish of St. Andrew Music Ministry.
